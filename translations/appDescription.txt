Based on the ANDv2 clock face with some customisations.

* 12/24 hour clock support
* AM/PM optional display on 12 hour clock
* Optional show/hide of seconds
* Date with day name
* Heartrate with optional animation & zone display
* Steps
* Distance
* Calories
* Floors
* Active Minutes
* BMI & BMR
* Progress bars for activity goals
* Battery bar along the top
* Optional battery percentage

Supported languages:
* English

To add support for your language, please provide translations to these files and email them or create a GitHub issues with them attached.
https://github.com/BlythMeister/Fitbit-Overview-Face/tree/master/translations
